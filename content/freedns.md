# Free domains and subdomains, no SMS required

[FreeDNS.afraid.org](/out/FreeDNS.afraid.org) offers free third-level domains and subdomains. Registration works with any email—I’d use something like @proton.me.

**What you can do:** Point an alias or subdomain at a server you’d rather not tie to your main domains—for example, one you paid for in crypto. Handy when you want a clean separation between that asset and the rest of your setup.

**What you can’t:** You won’t be able to point a CNAME from there to a hosted platform like Netlify. The service isn’t built for that. Not ideal if that’s the goal, but for simple aliasing it does the job.
